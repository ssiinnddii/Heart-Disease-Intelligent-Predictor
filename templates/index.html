KEY FACTS
Heart disease causes 17.9 million deaths annually — the world's leading cause of death.
Up to 80% of premature heart disease is preventable through lifestyle changes.

Understanding Your Risk

Heart disease risk is shaped by a combination of clinical indicators. Our model analyzes these measurements and shows you exactly which factors matter most.

Risk Level Probability Guidance
Low < 35% Maintain healthy habits, regular check-ups.
Medium 35 – 65% Discuss results with your doctor.
High > 65% Seek cardiac evaluation promptly.
