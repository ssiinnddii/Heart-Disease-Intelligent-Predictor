🤖 Machine Learning Pipeline

The model is a scikit-learn Pipeline that chains preprocessing and classification into a single, serialisable object. Using a Pipeline means the exact same transformations applied during training are automatically applied at inference time – preventing data leakage and keeping training and serving consistent.

Step 1 – Numeric preprocessing
Median imputation (for missing values) → Standard scaling (mean=0, std=1)
Step 2 – Categorical preprocessing
Mode imputation → Ordinal encoding (safe for tree models)
Step 3 – Gradient Boosting
100 estimators, random_state=42. Best F1 across 6 tested algorithms.
